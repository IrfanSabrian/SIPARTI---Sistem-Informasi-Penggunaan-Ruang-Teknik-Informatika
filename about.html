About Us

Welcome! to Classroom Schedule

Your number one source for managing classroom schedules seamlessly. We're dedicated to providing you with the very best experience, focusing on simplicity, reliability, and efficiency.

Our Journey

Founded in 2024 by students of informatics engineering program of Pontianak State Polytechnic class 4E, Classroom Schedule has come a long way from its beginnings in Pontianak, Kalimantan Barat. When we first started out, our passion for simplifying classroom scheduling drove us to start this project so that Classroom Schedule can offer you a user-friendly platform.

Our Vision

We aim to be the leading platform for classroom scheduling.
